Spring & Fall Lawn Cleanup in Gretna & Omaha

Get your yard back in shape after winter — or before it

Every lawn needs two big resets each year: one in spring, one in fall. Skip either and next season starts behind. We handle the full cleanup — trimming, raking, bed prep, weed removal, shrub pruning, and leaf removal — in one visit so you don't have to spend a weekend on it.

What's Included

  • Overgrown grass trimming — for first-visit or neglected lawns
  • Landscaping weed removal from beds and around hardscape
  • Shrub pruning (seasonal appropriate)
  • Leaf removal — bagged, hauled, or mulched depending on your preference
  • Bed cleanup and edging for clean, defined lines
  • Debris haul-away — we take it with us

Spring Cleanup Focus

Spring cleanup is about un-doing winter and setting the lawn up for the season ahead. Typical spring work:

  • Lift and remove matted winter grass to open the canopy
  • Assess winter damage — snow mold, vole damage, salt burn
  • Bed cleanup, first edging of the year
  • Coordination with pre-emergent timing (we hit the window)
  • Clear out storm debris from branches and sticks

Best time for spring cleanup in the Omaha metro: late March to early April, once the ground has thawed and dried out. Details in our spring cleanup checklist.

Fall Cleanup Focus

Fall cleanup gets the lawn buttoned up for winter so it bounces back strong in spring:

  • Leaf collection and removal (heavy leaf loads smother cool-season grass)
  • Final mow height adjustment
  • Bed winterization
  • Perennial cutback and dead material removal
  • Shrub pruning on appropriate varieties

One-Time vs. Regular Customers

Cleanup is available both ways:

  • One-time cleanup — perfect if you just moved in, had a busy year, or need a reset
  • Included in seasonal packages — regular mowing customers get priority spring and fall cleanup scheduling

Service Areas

Spring and fall cleanup is available throughout Gretna, Omaha, Papillion, La Vista, Bellevue, Elkhorn, and the surrounding metro.
