Lawn Care in Elkhorn, NE

Larger lots, newer homes — the full service for west metro

Elkhorn newer subdivisions and larger lot sizes mean lawn care here is often a bigger project than in other parts of the metro. Quarter-acre and half-acre lots are common. Landscape beds are larger. Mowing takes longer. We are set up for it — with commercial-grade equipment that handles Elkhorn-sized properties efficiently and consistently.

Elkhorn Lawn Care Is Different

A few things set Elkhorn apart:

  • Larger lots mean more visible issues. A thin patch does not hide as easily when your lawn is ¾ acre vs. a quarter acre. Our fertilization and aeration programs maintain consistency across the full property.
  • More landscape beds. Elkhorn homes typically have extensive bed areas — which means more mulching, edging, and weed control are needed.
  • Newer construction = worse baseline soil. Like other west metro areas, much of Elkhorn sits on stripped topsoil. Aeration and organic matter building are long-term priorities.
  • Wind exposure. Open Elkhorn lots see more wind than central Omaha — which dries out lawns faster and makes watering timing more important.

Services Available

Full service lineup for Elkhorn homes:

  • Weekly Lawn Mowing — trimming, edging, and blowing off hard surfaces every visit
  • 6-Step Fertilization & Weed Control — pre-emergent, balanced feeding, and winterizer
  • Core Aeration & Over-Seeding — fall service for healthier, thicker turf
  • Mulch & Rock Installation — delivery, bed edging, and weed barrier
  • Spring & Fall Lawn Cleanup — leaf removal, debris haul-away, bed prep

Built for Larger Properties

We use commercial-grade equipment and calibrate pricing to lot size — so Elkhorn homeowners are not paying the same flat rate as a quarter-acre lot in Papillion. You get honest pricing based on actual service time, and visible edging and trim work on every visit.

For homes with larger landscape beds, our mulch & rock installation service is often a worthwhile one-time investment that significantly reduces weekly maintenance time.
