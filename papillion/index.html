Lawn Care in Papillion, NE

Fixing builder-grade lawns and keeping Papillion yards thriving

Papillion is one of the Omaha metro fastest-growing communities — which means a lot of new construction, a lot of new homeowners, and a lot of "builder grass" lawns that need help establishing. We specialize in transforming thin, patchy new-construction lawns into the thick, healthy turf every homeowner wants.

The New-Construction Lawn Problem

If you bought a home in Papillion in the last 5 years, there is a good chance your lawn looks like this:

  • Thin, stressed grass struggling through summer
  • Bare spots that never seem to fill in
  • More weeds than you would expect for a "new" lawn
  • Drainage issues or pooling water after rain

This is not you — it is the soil. During construction, builders strip the topsoil, grade with clay subsoil, and then spread a thin layer back on top. The result is grass growing on compacted, nutrient-poor ground. Our approach for Papillion new-construction lawns is a one-two punch:

  • Fall aeration + over-seeding to break up compaction and thicken existing turf
  • 6-step fertilization program to correct nutrient deficiencies over 2 to 3 seasons
